our story

Agustarello was first opened in 1957, founded by Agostino "Agustarello" Commentucci. The slaughterhouse workers came to play cards, drink a mezzolitro of red wine and savor the dishes prepared by Sor Agusto.

our family

Today, Agostino's heirs manage the restaurant that keeps the tradition of Roman cuisine alive, an experience that goes beyond food, taking you back to a vanished Rome.

Menu

Our specialities are those of the Quinto Quarto. The poorest parts of beef, which a skilled hand and decades of experience have transformed into dishes rich in flavour and difficult to find elsewhere.
